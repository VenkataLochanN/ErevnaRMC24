From data to decision - Internal medicine workshop

Conducted by:Department of General medicine, Rangaraya Medical College

“From data to decision” is designed to help healthcare professionals interpret laboratory data and apply it to real-world clinical scenarios. This aims to enhance diagnostic accuracy and clinical decision-making through interactive learning and practical applications.
