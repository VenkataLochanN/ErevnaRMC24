ADR Reporting and Analgesic drug evaluation

Conducted by:Department of Pharmacology, Rangaraya Medical College

In this pharmacology workshop, participants learn about reporting Adverse Drug Reactions (ADRs) and evaluating analgesics. They focus on recognizing, documenting, and managing unexpected medication side effects, as well as assessing the effectiveness and safety of pain-relief drugs.

Contents of the workshop:

1. ADR reporting by Vigiflow
2. Evaluation of analgesic on volunteer.
