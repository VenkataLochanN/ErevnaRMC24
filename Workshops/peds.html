Essentials of Neonatology

Conducted by: Department of Pediatrics, Rangaraya Medical College

Embark on a transformative learning experience with "Essentials of Neonatology," a handson workshop presented by the Department of Pediatrics! In this dynamic workshop, participants will have the opportunity to learn through hands-on experience with provided mannequins, gaining practical skills that will be invaluable in their future medical practice.

Contents of the workshop:

1. Neonatal Resuscitation
2. Neonatal Jaundice
3. Feeding of pre-terms techniques
4. Routine care of newborn
