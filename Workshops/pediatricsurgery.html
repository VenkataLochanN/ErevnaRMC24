Pediatric Surgery

Conducted by: Department of Pediatric Surgery, Rangaraya Medical College

This workshop offers students hands on to learn key techniques for establishing vascular access, including the use of central lines, peripheral IVs, and advanced catheter placements, ensuring effective management of surgical patients.

Contents of the workshop:

1. - Demonstration of various means of vascular Access
2. Demonstration of various vascular access devices
3. - Hands on procedures
