About Rangaraya Medical College

Rangaraya Medical College (RMC) is a prestigious medical institution located in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1958, it is named after the renowned philanthropist and freedom fighter, Dr. Rangaraya Naidu. The college is affiliated with Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences and is recognized by the Medical Council of India.

RMC has a rich history of providing quality medical education and healthcare services. It offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS) programs in various specialties. The college boasts a well-equipped campus with state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, and lecture halls to facilitate a conducive learning environment.

With a dedicated faculty of experienced professors, RMC emphasises a holistic approach to medical education, focusing on academic excellence, research, and compassionate patient care. The college has a teaching hospital attached to it, providing students with hands-on clinical experience.

Rangaraya Medical College has produced numerous skilled healthcare professionals who have made significant contributions to the medical field. It continues to uphold its commitment to producing competent doctors who can serve the healthcare needs of the community and contribute to the advancement of medical science.

About RSRS

Rangaraya Student Research Society(RSRS) is a student-led committee aiming to provide aid, support and a sense of collectivity for the young minds interested in research work. With its worthy committee members it has achieved various endeavours when it comes to conducting quiz competitions, conferences, guest lectures and research. Every member works towards one aim of letting the students of Rangaraya Medical College know there is more to studying medicine than books.
