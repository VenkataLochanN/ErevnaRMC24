About Erevna:

Greetings esteemed colleagues,

Rangaraya Medical College proudly presents EREVNA 3.0, the pinnacle of academic excellence and professional advancement in the medical realm. Building upon the resounding success of its predecessor, EREVNA 3.0 promises an unparalleled experience marked by cutting-edge workshops, stimulating competitions and extravaganza culturals.

Prepare to immerse yourselves in a symphony of knowledge and innovation as we delve into uncharted territories of medical expertise. Engage with esteemed professionals, exchange insights, and forge enduring alliances with peers hailing from diverse backgrounds across the nation.

Join us as we embark on a journey to elevate our collective medical acumen to unprecedented heights. EREVNA 3.0 awaits, poised to empower and inspire the next generation of medical luminaries.
