Rules:

Eligibility :
 1) All the Undergraduate MBBS students and Interns.

Rounds:
 1) The competition consists of three rounds in total: two rounds of a spelling bee and a final round of medimages.

Medical Spelling Bee
 1) There is a preliminary written round followed by an oral round.

 2) In the preliminary round, participants are given TEN words to spell within TWELVE minutes.

 3) Exceeding the time limit results in disqualification.

 5) A point is awarded for each correctly spelled word.

 6) The top 10 participants will advance to the next stage. There will not be a tie breaking round. For example, if three participants are tied for 9th place, a total of 11 participants will proceed to the next stage.

 7) In the oral round, each participant is given three words to spell.
 Three words are randomly selected from a box of slips. The slips are then handed to the pronouncer. The timer starts when the first word is pronounced.

 SPELLERS CAN NOT CHANGE THE SEQUENCE OF LETTERS THAT ARE ALREADY SPOKEN.

 9) The top three finalists are selected based on the number of correctly spelled words and the shortest completion time.

 Pronounciation: Each word is pronounced by the pronouncer.

 Spellers can ask for the word to be repeated, defined.

 Mind the time: Every second counts, including the time taken for definitions and word repetitions.

MedImages:
 1) The Final round is a buzzer round.
 2) Each question consists of a set of images. Participants need to guess the name of the drug based on these images.
 3) A total of 10 questions are displayed. The one who presses the buzzer first gets to answer the question.
 4) The participant with the most number of correct guesses is pronounced as the winner.
