Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation: EREVNA 2024

Rules:

1. Delegates registering for a research paper presentation must submit their original work
2. All students from first to final year are eligible to participate (interns are also eligible) and all participants shall receive a certificate of participation
3. One student can submit more than one abstract but the best one shall be selected
4. Paper presentation can be done in teams of two or individually, however only one person is allowed to present the paper
5. Members of the team can belong to the same or different colleges
6. Questions will be posed to the team as a whole and either of the members can answer them 7. Delegates are expected to send their abstracts before the deadline- 30th june 2024
8. All abstracts will be screened by a team of senior faculty members and the final contestant list shall be uploaded to the mail address registered by 4th july 2024
9. The decision taken by the faculty remains final
10. Delegates whose abstracts have been selected will be given prior intimation
11. The abstract should be submitted in the form of a word document only and must not exceed 300 words
12. The abstract must contain details of the author, co-author, department of study, institution of study and registration ID
13. The abstract file must contain text in English only, font: Times New Roman, size: 12, and line spacing: 1.5. references to be mentioned in Vancouver style 14. The abstract should be submitted in the following format:

  A. Title
  B. Introduction
  C. Aims and objectives
  D. Background
  E. Methodology
  F. Results
  G. Analysis
  H. Conclusion
  I. References

15. The submitted abstracts must be attested by the respective guide and the head of the department under whom research has been done
16. Informed consent form, case study form and questionnaire forms must be submitted along with the abstract if used for the study
17. It is not mandatory to submit IEC approval
18. The selected abstracts are eligible for oral presentation on 13th july 2024
19. The final presentation must be in the form of a PowerPoint presentation and must be submitted by 10th July 2024 to the respective coordinators
20. The presentation must not exceed 20 slides
21. Each presenter will be given 10 minutes- 7 minutes for the presentation and 3 minutes for the questionnaire
22. Winners will be declared during the valedictory ceremony
23. Not adhering to the above-mentioned rules can lead to disqualification of the team
24. Prizes include winning certificates,1st prize and 2nd prize trophy,cash prize of
