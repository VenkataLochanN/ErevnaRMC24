Medical Debate

Debate Competition: EREVNA 2024

Rules:

1. Delegates registering for debate must remember that debate is a team event.
2. Teams will be randomly assigned on the day of debate based on the registrations received.
3. The topic will be revealed on 10th July evening.
4. Remember that debate is a healthy argument, and any use of abusive or aggressive language or gestures is strictly not encouraged.
5. Teams will form and be informed a few days prior to the competition.
6. A prelim round may be held based on the number of participants.
7. You can choose to speak for or against the topic after you win the toss.
8. You will be judged based on content, organization, articulation and how well you justify your points.
9. Prizes will be given to the best team and best speaker.
10. prizes include winning certificates, trophies for best team and best speaker, cash prize for best team, for best speaker.
11. All participants will be given a certificate of participation.

Time: July 13th afternoon 2pm to 5pm

Venue: biochemistry gallery
