Poster Presentation: EREVNA 2024

Rules:

1. Poster presentations can be done in a team or individually
The team must consist of a maximum of three members
2. Only one person is allowed to present the poster, however the questions can be answered by all the members of the team 3. Poster can be designed either digitally or manually
4. The posters must be sent to the respective coordinators in PDF format only on or before 2nd July 2024
5. All information within the poster must appear in a single slide
6. Poster must be within the size limits of 3ft Ã— 4ft
7. Posters shall be screened and those selected for final presentation will be announced on 9th July 2024
8. Selected posters are eligible for presentation on 12th July 2024
9. The final posters whether designed manually or digitally must be brought to the venue
10. Each team will be given a total of 10 minutes- 5 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for the questionnaire
11. Prizes will be given for each topic separately and for the best poster, best presenter individually
(based on the number of participants) 12. All participants will be given certificates of participation and winners shall be awarded during the valedictory ceremony
