Anatomical Artistry

Registration Fee : 49/-

13th July, 9AM - 12 PM

Venue: Exam Hall

● These are two competitions that occur simultaneously and participants are required to choose only one.

1. Anatomy Body Painting

2. Histo Rangoli

Anantomy Body Painting : EREVNA 2024

Rules:

1. Eligibility:
  Participants must be undergraduate MBBS students or interns.

2. Team Composition
  Participants can compete individually or in teams of two( excluding your model).

3. Time Limit
  Each team is allotted 1 hour and 30 minutes to complete their painting.

4. Materials and Models
  Participants must bring their own supplies and arrange for their own male model for body painting. Participants should come prepared for relevant questions based on your painting.

5. Attire
  Only male models are preferred. Models must be in appropriate attire to maintain decorum. The lower limb should not be exposed above the knee. The chest and upper back can be used for painting.

6. Reference:
  No reference material is allowed during the competition.

Histo Rangoli : EREVNA 2024

Rules:

● In this competition, participants have to create rangoli based on histology diagrams.

1. Eligibility :
 Participants must be undergraduate MBBS students or interns.

2. Team Composition :
  There should be 3 participants in each team

3. Number of Rounds :
 There will be only one round of competition

4 Time Limit:
 Each team is allotted 1 hour and 30 minutes to complete their painting.

5. Materials:
  Only pink and purple/ violet rangoli colours must be used and participants must bring their own supplies.

6. Reference:
  No reference material is allowed during the competition.

● Prizes: There are separate prizes for each competition. Prizes incldue Winning certificate, Trophies for winning team and Cash Prize of Rs 1500/-
