Medical Quiz

Medical Quiz : EREVNA 2024

Rules:

1. Eligibility:
  a. The quiz competition is open to participants from 1st year to internship, including students from different colleges.
  b. Each team must consist of four members.
  c. The number of participants from the internship/final year should not exceed two per team.

2. Finals:
  a. Five teams will qualify for the finals.
  b. The finals will consist of six rounds, as described below.

3. Round Structure:
  a. Each round will focus on a specific year of study or subject.
  b. The rounds will be conducted in the following sequence:
    i. 1st round: First-year subjects (Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry)
    ii. 2nd round: Second-year subjects (Pharmacology, Pathology, Microbiology)
    iii. 3rd round: Third-year subjects(Opthalmology, ENT, PSM, FMT)
    iv. 4th round: Final-year subjects(Surgery,Orthopedics,Medicine,Obs&Gyn, Pediatrics)
    v. 5th round: Integrated round covering all subjects of MBBS
    vi. Final round: Rapid-fire round
  ALL QUESTIONS IN ROUNDS (1,2,3,4,5 ) ARE MCQs EXCEPT IN RAPID FIRE ROUND

4. Scoring System for Rounds 1-5:
  a. Each correct answer in rounds 1-5 will be awarded 20 marks.
  b. If a team chooses to pass a question and another team answers it correctly, then the answered team will receive 10 marks.
  c. Answering a question incorrectly will result in a deduction of 10 marks.
  d. Answering a passed question incorrectly will result in a deduction of 5 marks.
  e. Unattempted questions will receive zero marks. ( including passed question )

5. Scoring System for Rapid-Fire Round:
  a. Each correct answer in the rapid-fire round will be awarded 10 marks.
  b. Incorrect or unattempted questions in the rapid-fire round will receive zero marks.

6. Passing Questions:
  a. In rounds 1-5, if a team chooses to pass a question, the next team in line will only have the opportunity to answer it.

7. Round Order:
  a. The first round will start with Team A, and subsequent rounds will proceed in alphabetical order (Team B, Team C, etc.)
  b. Each team will get 1 question from each subject in rounds 1,2,3 and 4.
  c. In integrated round(5th round), each team will get 2 questions.

8. Rapid-Fire Round Format:
  a. In the final round, each team will have 1 min to answer a maximum of 10 rapid-fire questions.
  b. Teams must provide their answers within the given time limit.
  c. Incorrect or unattempted questions will not be carried forward.
  d. each correct answer will earn the team 10 points. incorrect answers will not deduce marks.

9. Tiebreaker:
  a. In the event of a tie, a tiebreaker question or round will be conducted to determine the winner.

10. Fair Play:
  a. Participants are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respect throughout the competition.
  b. Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or disruptive behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
