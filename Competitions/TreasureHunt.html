Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt: EREVNA 2024

Registration Fee : 199/-

Date and Time: 13th July, 7- 8:30 AM

Venue : RMC Main Campus

Rules:

1) Participation rules :

● Eligibility : All Undergraduate MBBS students and Interns are eligible.
● Teams : Each team can have 3-4 members .
● Registration : Teams must register and provide a name.


2) Game rules :

● All teams will start from a common point on campus
● There is a time limit for the entire hunt
● Teams must adhere to all campus safety regulations
● No disruptive or running behavior is allowed
● Use of electronic devices is prohibited


3) Clue and task regulations :

● Clues will relate to medical knowledge, college history or campus landmarks
● Clues will vary in difficulty
● At each checkpoint a coordinator will verify the completion of clue before providing the next one.
● Incorrect answers and missed clues may incur penalties.


4) Code of Conduct:

● Participants must respect each other, the coordinators, and college property.
● No collaboration between teams is allowed.
● Fair play: Teams should not tamper with or remove clues.


5) Winning Criteria-
● The first team to correctly solve all clues and reach the final point wins.
● If no team finishes within the time limit, the team with the most correctly solved clues wins.
● In case of a tie, a tie-breaker challenge is conducted.


6)Prizes :
● Prizes for the winning team include Winning Certificate, trophies and Cash Prize of Rs. 5000/-
● Participation certificates for all teams
